# Log Entry Format

Append one entry to `docs/{feature}/{feature}.log.md` after completing each slice.

```markdown
## `{slice-slug}` — {YYYY-MM-DD}

**Status:** done | blocked | needs-review
**Summary:** {What was built. One or two sentences.}
**Deviations:** {Any deviations from the plan, or "none".}
**Notes:** {Anything useful for the next developer — gotchas, decisions made, things to watch.}
```

Keep entries factual and brief. They feed the QA plan — focus on decisions that have downstream consequences and anything that got weird.
